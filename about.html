Covenant Crest Group Ltd is a multi-division UK company providing specialist recruitment, professional road haulage, and global food import & distribution services from our base in Telford, Shropshire.

Our Story

Covenant Crest Group Ltd (Company No. 16528951) was founded with one clear mission: to deliver genuine value across three sectors that are fundamental to the UK economy — people, logistics, and supply chains.

We believe that the best businesses are built not on transactions, but on relationships grounded in honesty, reliability, and a commitment to doing things properly. That is what “built on promise” means to us — not just a tagline, but a standard we hold ourselves to every day.

Based in Telford, Shropshire, we serve businesses and candidates across the UK, and partner with suppliers and buyers across international markets, with a particular focus on trade with India.
