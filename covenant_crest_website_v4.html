Covenant Crest Group Ltd

Covenant Crest Group Ltd is a UK registered multi-division limited company headquartered in Telford, Shropshire. We operate across four key sectors — recruitment, road haulage, import and distribution, and facilities management.

Founded in 2025 and led by Jaby Kamidi, every division is driven not just by commercial ambition but by a genuine commitment to excellence, integrity and the communities we serve.
